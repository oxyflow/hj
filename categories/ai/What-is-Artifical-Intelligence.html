What is Artifical Intelligence?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is one of the most revolutionary technologies advancing our world today. This article will look at AI and everything it encompasses, such as its potential applications for business, healthcare, education and even more complex fields like cybersecurity.

The Basics

Understanding how AI works: At a basic level, artificial intelligence involves machines processing data to make decisions based on instructions coded into algorithms by humans. It enables these machines or computer systems to perform functions usually reserved for human beings – tasks that require decision making and reasoning skills.

Types of AI

There are two main types of artificial intelligence; narrow/limited AI which focuses on performing specific tasks within certain pre-programmed parameters e.g., facial recognition software; while general/strong AI applies advanced problem solving techniques across many different disciplines including language understanding, visual perception etc., self driving cars being an example here.
